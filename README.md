## Overview

CyberCrowd Net is a discovery-first live media and camera-access platform built to support live culture, creator/client broadcasts, MultiCam experiences, archives, accessibility expansion, coordinated digital interaction, and income independence for both viewers and creators.

CyberCrowd is not a basic static website or simple storefront. It is being developed as a platform ecosystem where viewers, subscribers, creators, clients, and device-based tools can connect through live media access, WDIG control, CyberShop products, MultiCam trials, and future Reten Records media integration.

The long-term goal is to create a system where creators can monetize their work, viewers can participate in platform value, clients can purchase camera/broadcast access, and the platform can support new digital income paths instead of leaving users as passive consumers.

## Purpose

CyberCrowd Net aims to connect audiences with engaging content and facilitate interactions around live cultural, entertainment, creator, and client-driven events.

The platform is designed to help users discover, participate in, and benefit from live experiences through camera-access tools, WDIG systems, MultiCam trials, CyberShop products, accessibility features, archives, and future media systems.

A central purpose of CyberCrowd is to support income independence.

For creators, CyberCrowd is intended to provide tools for exposure, monetization, camera packages, broadcast participation, audience access, and future platform revenue opportunities.

For viewers, CyberCrowd is intended to move beyond passive watching by creating paths for participation, discovery, rewards, access-based interaction, and future income-connected opportunities as the platform expands.

CyberCrowd’s mission is to build a media platform where live culture, technology, accessibility, and monetization work together so both creators and viewers can gain more independence, opportunity, and value from digital participation.
