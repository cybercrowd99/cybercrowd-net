# CyberCrowd / RetenRecords Web Foundation

This repository supports development for CyberCrowd.net and RetenRecords. The official public platform home is CyberCrowd.net.

## Overview

CyberCrowd Net is a discovery-first live media, camera-access, advertising, accessibility, safety, creator-income, viewer-participation, and digital platform ecosystem.

CyberCrowd is not a basic static website, simple storefront, or single-purpose media page. It is being developed as a larger platform environment where viewers, subscribers, creators, clients, advertisers, accessibility users, families, communities, and device-based tools can connect through live media access, WDIG control, MultiCam trials, CyberShop products, adWorm exposure systems, Magic Cursor interaction, Reten Records media, and future CRWD platform currency systems.

The long-term goal is to create a platform where creators can monetize their work, viewers can participate in platform value, clients can purchase camera/broadcast access, advertisers can gain exposure through coordinated ad systems, and accessibility-focused tools can help users interact with digital spaces more safely and independently.

CyberCrowd is designed to become a coordinated platform for live culture, discovery, media, access, safety, advertising, income independence, and user-controlled digital experience.

## Purpose

CyberCrowd Net aims to connect audiences with engaging content and facilitate interactions around live cultural, entertainment, creator, client-driven, music, news, archive, and community experiences.

The platform is designed to help users discover, participate in, and benefit from live experiences through camera-access tools, WDIG systems, MultiCam trials, CyberShop products, ad exposure systems, accessibility features, archives, Reten Records media, and future platform currency systems.

A central purpose of CyberCrowd is to support income independence.

For creators, CyberCrowd is intended to provide tools for exposure, monetization, camera packages, broadcast participation, audience access, ad-supported visibility, Reten Records media publishing, and future platform revenue opportunities.

For viewers, CyberCrowd is intended to move beyond passive watching by creating paths for participation, discovery, rewards, access-based interaction, possible future CRWD participation value, and income-connected opportunities as the platform expands.

For clients, CyberCrowd is intended to support camera packages, live-event participation, broadcast setups, promotional exposure, and hosted digital experiences.

For advertisers and sponsors, CyberCrowd is intended to support exposure through adWorm-style perimeter advertising, page-based promotional space, live-event placements, sponsor visibility, and future coordinated digital ad experiences.

For accessibility users, CyberCrowd is intended to support future tools such as Blind Slate, Deaf Companion, de-escalation support, Magic Cursor interaction, and device-based assistance systems.

CyberCrowd’s mission is to build a media platform where live culture, technology, accessibility, advertising, safety, and monetization work together so creators, viewers, clients, sponsors, and users with accessibility needs can gain more independence, opportunity, and value from digital participation.

## Platform Layers

CyberCrowd is being built around several connected layers.

### CyberCrowd.net

Main public platform, discovery pages, CyberShop, WDIG access information, search-indexed public entry point, and future platform portal.

### WDIG

Device-based host/control layer for future pairing, access, camera switching, local control, Magic Cursor, Surface OS, kiosk logic, and platform tools.

### CyberShop

Access point for CyberCrowd products, including the WDIG Access Pass, future MultiCam trial access, platform access products, and future CyberCrowd/Reten Records items.

### MultiCam

Temporary or subscription-based expanded camera access for viewers, creators, clients, shows, broadcasts, and events.

### adWorm

Advertising exposure layer for perimeter ads, sponsor placement, page-based promotion, creator/client exposure, event visibility, and future monetization systems.

### Reten Records

Future music/media home for audio, teasers, covers, videos, playlists, artist media, music videos, and release media.

### Magic Cursor

Future interaction/control layer for page navigation, live media control, accessibility interaction, chat control, device pairing, and coordinated user experiences.

### Blind Slate

Future accessibility concept for blind and low-vision users, designed around simplified interaction, audio-assisted access, tactile/digital flow, and user-supportive navigation.

### Deaf Companion

Future accessibility concept for deaf and hard-of-hearing users, designed around visual alerts, captions, interaction prompts, safety signals, and coordinated support features.

### De-escalation Tools

Future safety/accessibility layer intended to support disabled users, caregivers, staff, families, or public-facing environments with calmer interaction flows, assistance prompts, and conflict-reduction support.

### Theft Locator

Future locator/safety concept intended for tracking, identifying, or recovering protected devices, assets, access tools, or platform-linked hardware.

### CRWD

Future CyberCrowd internal token/currency concept for platform value, participation, access, rewards, or internal ecosystem transactions.

### Atomic Kricket

Future truth/news platform concept for verified media, news discovery, public-interest content, commentary, and platform-supported information access.

### Surface OS

Future device/surface interaction system for kiosk-style control, paired devices, hosted access, Magic Cursor interaction, and local platform environments.

## adWorm Advertising Layer

adWorm is part of the CyberCrowd platform direction.

The adWorm concept supports advertising exposure around CyberCrowd pages, broadcasts, discovery areas, live media experiences, CyberShop products, Reten Records media, creator/client environments, and future platform experiences.

adWorm is intended to become a perimeter advertising and promotional exposure system that can support:

- Page-based ad exposure
- Perimeter advertising zones
- Creator/client promotional placements
- Sponsor visibility
- Event-based ad placements
- CyberShop product exposure
- Reten Records media promotion
- Future viewer/creator revenue opportunities
- Future platform income support

The goal of adWorm is not to interrupt the platform experience, but to create coordinated ad exposure that can support monetization, sponsorship, discovery, and platform income.

adWorm should remain separate from the WDIG Access Pass.

WDIG is the host/control access tool.

adWorm is the advertising and exposure layer.

CyberShop is the product/access layer.

MultiCam is the camera trial/subscription layer.

Reten Records is the future music/media layer.

CRWD is the future internal platform currency/token layer.

## Accessibility and Safety Direction

CyberCrowd is intended to include accessibility and safety systems as the platform grows.

Future accessibility and safety concepts include:

- Blind Slate
- Deaf Companion
- Magic Cursor accessibility control
- De-escalation support
- Visual/audio assistance prompts
- Device pairing support
- Kiosk/surface interaction
- Caregiver/staff support flows
- Theft Locator
- Safer device-linked access tools

Blind Slate is a future concept intended to help blind and low-vision users interact with CyberCrowd tools, media, navigation, and device-linked experiences.

Deaf Companion is a future concept intended to help deaf and hard-of-hearing users receive visual support, alerts, captions, prompts, and platform interaction assistance.

De-escalation support is intended to help reduce conflict, confusion, or unsafe interaction patterns in public-facing, accessibility, care, or device-supported environments.

Theft Locator is a future concept intended to help protect devices, platform assets, access tools, or connected systems that may be lost, stolen, or misplaced.

These tools are part of the larger CyberCrowd direction: access, independence, safety, and coordinated digital support.

## CRWD Platform Currency / Token Direction

CRWD is a future internal CyberCrowd token/currency concept.

CRWD may eventually support:

- Internal platform value
- Viewer participation rewards
- Creator support
- Access credits
- Event participation
- CyberShop utility
- Ad exposure systems
- Future Atomic Kricket systems
- Future CyberCrowd ecosystem transactions

CRWD should be treated as a future platform concept, not as the first build requirement.

The first build should focus on stable pages, indexing, Stripe access products, WDIG Access Pass structure, CyberShop readiness, and R2 media organization.
