CyberCrowd Asset Discovery

CyberCrowd Asset Discovery is the creator, client, talent, content, campaign, surface, and platform intake lane for CyberCrowd’s rotating platform ecosystem.

Asset Discovery Purpose

CyberCrowd is not a linear feed where everything is uploaded into the same stream. Asset Discovery helps identify what something is, who it belongs to, where it should go, how it may be used, and what system should review or route it.

What CyberCrowd May Discover

  • Creators
  • Clients
  • Producers
  • Performers
  • Speakers and public voices
  • Ad authors and campaign creators
  • Show concepts
  • Live event concepts
  • Camera package opportunities
  • Viewer upgrade opportunities
  • Public discourse topics
  • Atomic Kricket segment sources
  • Reten Records media or stage arts references
  • adWorm placement opportunities
  • Platform proof objects

Content Intake

Content intake may include videos, images, campaign ideas, show notes, live session proposals, public discourse topics, client requests, creator pitches, surface ideas, adWorm placements, and archive-worthy proof objects.

Creator and Client Intake

CyberCrowd may use Asset Discovery to identify creators, clients, venues, operators, producers, advertisers, and approved users who should be routed toward creator control, cam packages, viewer upgrades, adWorm, live rooms, or platform review.

Talent and Skill Discovery

Asset Discovery may reveal people with useful skills: live production, campaign writing, ad placement design, public discourse moderation, show hosting, camera operation, performance, content review, or platform support.

Review and Recall Workflow

CyberCrowd may later support review and recall workflows. A creator, client, campaign, surface, proof object, or content proposal may be saved, denied, reviewed again, recalled, routed, archived, or connected to a live event.

Connection to WDIG

WDIG asks where something belongs. Asset Discovery identifies the thing before WDIG routes it. Together, they help prevent files, people, campaigns, signals, and proof objects from being thrown into the wrong surface.

Connection to adWorm

Asset Discovery may feed adWorm when a placement, sponsor message, PSA, product notice, campaign idea, or ad author belongs in the paid publication or enterprise campaign system.

Connection to Public Discourse

Public topics, debate signals, societal sentiment, confusion clusters, and audience questions may enter CyberCrowd through Asset Discovery before being routed toward Public Discourse, Transparency Mode, Boardroom, or Archive.

Connection to Reten Records

Reten Records is the music, video, stage arts, records, release, and catalog vessel. Some discovered assets may belong in Reten’s world, while CyberCrowd remains the sandbox, control layer, and routing platform.

Connection to the CyberCrowd System

Asset Discovery may connect to WDIG Routing, Magic Cursor, adWorm Rotator, Cam Packages, Viewer Upgrades, Creator Control, Live Control Room, Public Discourse, Societal Sentiment, Platform Archive, Atomic Kricket, Reten Records, Access Passes, and Secure Surface Hardware.
