CyberCrowd Replay & VOD Access

CyberCrowd Replay & VOD Access defines how live events may become post-event viewing experiences, replay windows, video-on-demand access points, archive references, or future paid unlocks.

Replay and VOD Purpose

A CyberCrowd live event should not vanish after the broadcast ends. Replay and VOD access can preserve event value while keeping viewing permissions clear, event-specific, and auditable.

Possible Replay Access Types

  • Free replay window
  • Limited-time replay access
  • Paid replay access
  • Subscriber replay access
  • Event-ticket replay access
  • Creator-approved replay access
  • Client-controlled replay access
  • Archive reference replay
  • VOD access after live event
  • Premium camera angle replay

Access Windows

Replay and VOD access may be controlled by time windows, event permissions, viewer upgrades, creator/client decisions, access passes, or future payment rules. The system should define what can be watched, by whom, and for how long.

Connection to Event Access

Event Access defines who can enter the live event. Replay and VOD Access defines who can return after the live event, what replay surfaces are available, and whether the replay is public, limited, premium, or archived.

Connection to Viewer Upgrades

Viewer Upgrades may unlock extra replay options such as additional camera angles, behind-the-scenes access, extended viewing windows, or premium VOD access.

Creator and Client Control

Creators and clients may need control over whether an event becomes replay content, which portions are available, which camera angles are included, and whether access is free, limited, private, or paid.

Archive and Proof

A replay may become part of the Platform Archive when it preserves event structure, access decisions, camera configuration, dispute evidence, client review material, or platform lessons.

Future Payment Connection

Replay and VOD access may later connect to Stripe, subscriptions, event purchases, access passes, or other payment systems. Payment wiring should only be added after replay access rules are clear.

Connection to the CyberCrowd System

Replay and VOD Access may connect to Event Access, Viewer Upgrades, Trial Access, Access Passes, Creator Control, Live Control Room, Cam Packages, Device Trust, WDIG Routing, Magic Cursor, Platform Archive, adWorm, Atomic Kricket, and Reten Records media events.
