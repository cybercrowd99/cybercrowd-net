CyberCrowd Rotating Platform

CyberCrowd is a rotating platform, not a linear feed. It is designed so attention, control, media, creators, viewers, proof, public discourse, and live surfaces can rotate into focus instead of being trapped in one endless timeline.

Not a Linear Feed

Traditional platforms often force activity into a straight line: upload, scroll, react, and disappear downstream. CyberCrowd is designed around a different structure. A live room, camera angle, public signal, archive proof object, creator surface, Reten Records media event, or Atomic Kricket segment can become the center when the system rotates toward it.

Rotating Platform Purpose

The rotating platform model helps CyberCrowd avoid flattening every person, file, event, surface, record, placement, and conversation into one feed. It gives the system a way to move attention where it belongs.

Where We Belong

Rotation depends on placement. CyberCrowd uses the Where We Belong logic to ask where a person, file, device, signal, surface, placement, record, or proof object belongs before it is moved into focus.

Connection to WDIG

WDIG helps identify and route what belongs where. The rotating platform model depends on WDIG because rotation without placement becomes chaos. WDIG gives rotation direction.

Connection to Magic Cursor

Magic Cursor supports movement and control across surfaces. In a rotating platform, Magic Cursor helps guide attention, select surfaces, coordinate inputs, and move between live system areas without flattening everything into a single screen.

Connection to adWorm

adWorm is the paid and approved rotating publication system. It can place approved messages, sponsor placements, PSAs, creator promotions, product placements, and campaign material into rotation across approved surfaces.

Connection to Atomic Kricket and Reten Records

Atomic Kricket can operate as a culture-magazine and show layer inside the rotating platform. Reten Records can operate as the music, video, stage arts, records, release, and catalog vessel. CyberCrowd coordinates the platform logic around both.
